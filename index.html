Overview

World War II was a conflict between 1939 and 1945 that involved all the world's major countries.
It was the most destructive war in history and millions of people were killed. It was fought between
the Axis (Germany, Japan, and Italy) and the Allies (Britain, the US, and the Soviet Union among others).
